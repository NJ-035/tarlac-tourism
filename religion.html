Explore Tarlac

RELIGION


Explore Tarlac Through Faith

Tarlac is home to a rich spiritual heritage, offering sacred sites and places of worship that reflect deep devotion. Visit the iconic Monasterio de Tarlac, housing a relic of the True Cross, or explore historic churches like Saint Josemaria Escriva Parish and San Sebastian Cathedral. Experience vibrant religious festivals, serene pilgrimage sites, and faith-driven traditions that bring communities together. Whether for reflection, history, or devotion, Tarlac welcomes every soul on a journey of faith.

La Sagrada Familia

Sulipa, Gerona, Tarlac, Philippines

The parish combines intricate elements of classical designs and the functionality of modern architecture. Other than its art, La Sagrada Familia is also a popular tourist destination as it houses a slab of the Papal table used in the 2015 Papal Mass in Luneta, Manila.

This destination is ideal for picnicking, hiking, and enjoying nature, making it a favored spot for local families and visitors seeking a relaxing day out. Charge your camera (or smartphone) first so you can take a lot of nature pictures here!


Overall, t’s also a great place for families and groups to relax, with designated areas for grilling and simple outdoor recreation. To fully enjoy the area, visitors can bring food, set up a picnic, and explore nearby natural attractions, making it a perfect getaway for a day trip.
